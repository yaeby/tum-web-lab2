Our Story

Masters of Automotive Imagery.

Our story begins in Chisinau, Moldova, where a group of passionate automotive photographers decided to create something unique. In the shadows, we capture what others miss—the raw power, the unfiltered speed, the essence of machines built for dominance. Monzza Group is not just a photography company; we are the unseen force behind the most captivating automotive visuals.

From high-stakes races to exclusive supercar unveilings, we document every moment with precision. Our lenses carve through the night, revealing the soul of the machine—its roar, its velocity, its defiance of limits. Some call it obsession. We call it art.
